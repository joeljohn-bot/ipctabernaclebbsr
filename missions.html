WINGS Tailoring Center

As a part of the skill development, we train young girls in tailoring. Many girls and women in village area succesfully completed their course and are now able to stand on their feet. This became a source of livelihood for many rural women.

Youth Groups

Youths are the cream and backbone of the society. Majority of our church members are students and youngsters. Through our Youth Ministry, we encourage and guide youngsters to share their concerns and help them to go more closer to God. Ocassionally, we conduct Youth Seminars and Retreat for their spiritual upliftment and wholistic development.

Discipleship Center

Leadership is voluntary but discipleship is mandatory. We conduct discipleship centres in different locations to equip local youths for witnessing Christ and studying the Word in the local language through short term courses.

Literature Ministries

We involve in distributing and promoting Christian literature in vernacular language through translating books to local language and putting stalls in different book fairs and exhibitions.

Evangelism
