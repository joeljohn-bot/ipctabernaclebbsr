Welcome to IPC Tabernacle, Bhubaneswar. We are glad, that you are here. We exist as a church to love the Lord Jesus Christ and to extend His Love to others. Our goal is to glorify the name of the Lord. We are a multi-lingual spirit filled and Bible believing church. We teach the Word of God, worship passionately and are willing to go out to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ..
