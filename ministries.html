VBS

We have Sunday School in English and Odia with three different grades. Experienced teachers are taking classes and giving training in songs, skits, etc. During summer holidays, we conduct VBS in selected towns and villages for the moral and spiritual upliftment of the children. Resources persons trained in children's ministry are giving leadership to this VBS Program.

Family Enrichment

In the present context, family disputes, divorces, broken families are very common. As solution to this problem, we conduct family seminars and workshops to build healthy families on the basis of biblical principles.

Christmas Events

Christmas meetings are conducted at different places during December every year.
